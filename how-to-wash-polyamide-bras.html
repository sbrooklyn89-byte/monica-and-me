Care Guide

Wash gently. Skip heat. Let elastic rest.

Polyamide bras are everyday pieces, but heat and over-wearing can be hard on elastic. A simple wash routine and weekly rotation can help them feel fresher longer.

Direct answer

The safest routine is cool wash, gentle handling and air dry.

Always follow the care label first. In general, bras last better when they avoid high heat, rough washing and daily repeat wear without rest.

  1. Close hooks before washing so they do not snag lace or mesh.
  2. Use cool water and gentle detergent.
  3. Use a mesh bag if machine washing is allowed by the care label.
  4. Reshape cups gently after washing.
  5. Air dry flat or hang from the center gore, not by stretching straps.

Why rotation matters.

Even clean bras need elastic recovery time. A five-bra rotation makes it easier to avoid wearing the same underwire bra every day, especially for fuller-figure shoppers who rely on band support.

Best for / not best for

Best for: shoppers who want an everyday bra set to stay fresher through the week.

Not best for: replacing the product care label. Always follow the specific Amazon/listing care instructions for the item you bought.
