Before You Buy

Frequently asked

How do I choose between the 1/2-cup and 3/4-cup silhouettes?
The 1/2-cup demi is the more revealing of the two — ideal for plunge necklines, cocktail dresses and sweetheart tops. The 3/4-cup offers fuller coverage and is our pick for T-shirts, button-down shirts and anyone who wants less reveal at the décolletage. Both use the same underwire engineering, so support is identical.
What is the fabric, and does it run hot?
Polyamide (nylon) with a small percentage of elastane for stretch. It's far more breathable than cotton/spandex blends at the same weight, and in the fuller-bust community it's widely preferred because it holds its shape wash after wash — no stretched-out cups after three months.
The set includes five colours — which ones?
Every 5-pack ships with Beige, Powder Rose, Lavender, Sage Mist and Noir. Each bra is individually wrapped inside the box so they arrive clean and ready to wear.
How do I know which size to order?
Measure snugly under the bust for the band (inches) and loosely across the fullest point for the cup. Subtract band from cup to get cup size: 3" = C, 4" = D. Our patterns run true to US size, and both the C and D cup are drafted on a fuller-bust block. If you're between bands, size up — the wings will settle after a wear.
How do I care for polyamide bras?
Delicate wash, cold, inside a mesh laundry bag. Air dry — never tumble. Clasp the hooks before washing to stop them catching on the lace. With this routine, expect 12–18 months of regular rotation from each bra in the set.
What is your return policy?
