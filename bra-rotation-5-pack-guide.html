5-Pack Rotation

Why rotating bras helps everyday support.

A fuller-bust bra works hard. Rotating bras through the week gives elastic time to rest and makes it easier to match cup finish to the outfit.

The simple reason

Elastic needs recovery time.

When a bra is worn all day, the band and wings stretch with body movement. For fuller-bust shoppers, the band does a lot of support work. Giving a bra a rest day helps it return closer to its original tension before the next wear.

A five-pack makes outfit planning easier.

Different outfits need different finishes. Fabric cups usually work better under smooth T-shirts. Lace cups or lace wings can be a good choice when texture is not an issue. A multi-bra rotation gives you more flexibility without rebuilding your drawer one bra at a time.

MONICA & ME fit note: the 5-pack includes Midnight Navy, Powder Rose, Lavender, Sage Mist and Noir. The six 25mm series combine fabric cups, lace cups, fabric wings and lace wings.

How to care for a weekly bra rotation.

Clasp hooks before washing, use a mesh bag, wash cold and air dry. Avoid tumble drying because heat can be tough on elastic and lace. If a bra feels damp after wear, let it fully dry before putting it back in the drawer.

When to replace everyday bras.

If the band rides up even on a tighter hook, the cups gape differently than before, or the elastic no longer recovers after washing, it may be time to replace or refresh the rotation.
