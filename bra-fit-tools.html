Free Fit Tools

Three quick checks before building an everyday bra rotation.

Use these tools to narrow the fit question before buying: check your size range, choose a color by outfit, then decide whether 1/2-cup or 3/4-cup coverage makes more sense.

Tool 01

38C-44C fit checklist.

Start with the parts that decide daily comfort before thinking about decoration.

  • Band stays level instead of riding up.
  • Underwire follows breast tissue without poking.
  • Cups sit smoothly without obvious gaps or overflow.
  • Coverage works with your most-worn necklines.
  • You have enough bras to rotate during the week.

Tool 02

Bra color matcher.

Choose color by shirt contrast, not by what looks brightest in the drawer.

  • White shirts: lower-contrast tones and smoother cups.
  • Black or navy tops: Noir or Midnight Navy.
  • Pastels: Powder Rose, Lavender or Sage Mist.
  • Textured tops: lace can work when show-through is not a concern.
Best next pageRead the color guide.
Tool 03

1/2-cup vs 3/4-cup selector.

Pick coverage by neckline and containment preference.

  • Choose 1/2-cup for lower necklines.
  • Choose 3/4-cup for more front coverage.
  • Choose fabric cups for smoother T-shirt outfits.
  • Choose lace cups or wings when texture is part of the look.
